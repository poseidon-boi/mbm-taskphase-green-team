Integrated Human Practices

Dr. Jonathan Müller

Principal Scientist (Strain Development) at Solena Materials Limited
Date- 10/1/26
LinkedIn

We reached out to Dr. Jonathan Muller to discuss his thesis on the synthesis of γ-decalactone and other terpenoids. Through this interaction we gained quite a few valuable insights into potential strategies for improving yield by identifying the bottlenecks he encountered. We also investigated whether altering these enzymes or using a different chassis could improve production after learning about specific mutations in BM3 and thioesterase. He also discussed some of the practical difficulties he encountered while conducting his experiments, which would have assisted us in creating our own strategy if we had chosen this project.

Dr. Zijay Tang

Co-Founder and CTO at Anthology
Date- 12/11/26
LinkedIn

As a part of our Human Practices, we reached out to Dr. Zijay Tang for one of our previous projects to gain insight to the practical limitations of the DEPCOS system for environmental sensing. He highlighted that the maintenance of cellular activity with continuous nutrient availability and proper temperature was the main factor that limited its lifetime. He also explained that alginate-based shells need a constant supply of ions like calcium to stay stable and avoid swelling. These insights helped us better understand the trade-offs between biosafety, material stability, and long-term deployment.
